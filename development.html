World Overview

The world of Azura is a dangerous and deadly place. The more skilled and advanced adventures often tell beginner adventures to not stray too far from civilization as the creatures get exponentially more difficult the further away you go and therefore you need to always be prepared. There are (currently) 5 major cities and around 11 small towns there are several different biomes more deadly than the last, these include, grasslands, forests, plains, deserts, swamps, mountains and the ocean with many different types of creatures living in there respective biomes.

Character Overview

You are a simple citizen of the Sharkary Village you parents were defenders of the village but passed away doing their duties and thus you have only learned the basics they know, Now at this current point the Champion of the Divine has fallen and Adventures form all over the continent have come to try to take is place. With your basic skills set off to become the new Champion of the Divine. Throughout this gurney your character will grow stronger with the more experience they have, and you may even find help on the way.But be wary not to trust everyone.
