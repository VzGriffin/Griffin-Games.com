My Inspiration

In my time playing video games there has yet to be a game that truly impressed me in the fantasy genre. The logic of such games make no sense for instance slicing at a creatures legs till it dies or just blindly hacking away as timing till it dies or my personal worst one where you can go in solo against a creature that's lived thousands of years trains all its life but you seemingly can beat it in one on one combat. These types of games are far too common and thus I wish to change that within my game.

Game's Inspiration

My main inspiration behind this game is largely based of the legend of Zelda games, Dungeon defenders one and Dragons Dogma.

Game style

The game style will be moderate but rather detailed polly, games like dragon's dogma for example. It will be an RPG open world game and contain several voice acted Characters, with quests lines ranging from comedic to tragic.

Healping hand

Several people will be helping me with this game, all being close friends such as Gererd Murphy who will be assisting me on character and monster modelling. And James Rama who will be lending his voice to several characters and creatures of the world.

How long whill it take?

I plan to try to release the game within the next 8 or so years as I am currently at University this will be rather difficult to do at the moment but when I get more free time this should be no issue.
